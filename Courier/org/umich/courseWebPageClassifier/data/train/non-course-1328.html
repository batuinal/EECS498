Dates & Deadlines - Thesis Writers

The following is a list of the important dates and deadlines for thesis writers and 2-semester IW work students.

AB Students: COS 981 - Junior IW two semester project / COS 984 Sr. Thesis

BSE Students: COS 497 & COS 498 Sr. Thesis

September 10 (✓ Completed)

Classes begin. Look here for project ideas.

September 11 (✓ Completed)

Thesis Signup Deadline.  Hard copies of your 2 semester/thesis signup forms are due, signed by your faculty advisor, in the UGC's office.

September 15, 4:30pm, CS 105 (✓ Completed)

Welcome meeting. Pizza will be served. Attendance is MANDATORY.

September 22 - 26, CS 210 (Deadline is 4:30pm Sep 25) (✓ Completed)

Sign ups for 2 semester IW/Thesis proposal presentations/progress reports will be done via WASS (Web Appointment Scheduling System). The UGC will provide the link to the calendars via email.

October 6 - 10, location TBD (✓ Completed)

Proposal presentations/progress reports given this week.

Please upload a .pdf of your presentation here:

https://dropbox.cs.princeton.edu/COSIW_F2014/Proposal_presentation

Upload a .pdf of your talk by 8am the morning of your talk. Please name your slide as follows:

DayOfPresentation_FirstInitial_Lastname.pdf

(EG for Maria Smith presenting on Wednesday it would be: Wednesday_M_Smith.pdf)

Students are expected to attend for an entire 1-hour block (starting and ending on the hour) and give feedback to other students in that time period.

Consult with your advisor in advance if they would like to attend.

→  November 7, 4:30pm, CS 210    (✓ Completed)

Fall Semester Checkpoint Report Form, signed by your advisor due to the UGC

E-mail a PDF copy of your checkpoint report to iwcoord (@lists.cs.princeton.edu) with a subject line of "FALL 2013 Checkpoint Report FirstName LastName" (with your name in place of FirstName LastName)

 

Thesis writers/2-semester IW work continues into the Spring 2015 semester:

AB Students: COS 981 - Junior IW two semester project / 984 Sr. Thesis

BSE Students: COS 497 & COS 498 Sr. Thesis

All two semester/thesis student are invited to attend the Fall IW poster session on

January 12, 2015 - Convocation Room, Friend Center

February 6, 4:30pm, CS 210 - Two forms and Feb. paper due    (✓ Completed)

Second Reader Form Due, signed by your second reader due to the UGC

February Paper & Form - Do the following three things:

(1) Have your advisor sign the form and give it to the UGC

(2) E-mail a PDF copy of your February paper to ckenny (@cs.princeton.edu) with a subject line of "February Paper FirstName LastName" (with your name in place of FirstName LastName)

(3) upload a .pdf of your paper here:

https://dropbox.cs.princeton.edu/COSIW_S2015/FebPaper

Please name your paper as follows:

Firstname_Lastname.pdf 

March 23, 4:30 pm, CS 210

Final Thesis Outline Due, signed by advisor, due to the UGC. The outline should be specific regarding your main results and contributions, and in whatever form your advisor deems suitable.

April 6-8 2015 CS 210 (Deadline is 4:30 pm)

Sign ups for IW/Thesis proposal presentations/progress reports will be done via WASS (Web Appointment Scheduling System). The UGC will provide the link to the calendars via email.

April 20 - 24

Final Presentations this week.

Please upload a .pdf of your presentation here:

https://dropbox.cs.princeton.edu/COSIW_S2015/FinalPresentation Upload a .pdf of your talk by 8am the morning of your talk. Please name your slide as follows:

DayOfPresentation_FirstInitial_Lastname.pdf

(EG for Maria Smith presenting on Monday it would be: Monday_M_Smith.pdf)

April 30, 4:30 pm

Final Report Due, PDF e-mailed to advisor/reader and (ckenny (@cs.princeton.edu)), and uploaded at

https://dropbox.cs.princeton.edu/COSIW_S2015/FinalPaper

One printed copy should be delivered to the UGC in room CS 210. Note: There should be no statement regarding access and copying. There should be a statement indicating that the thesis is your own work in accordance with University regulations.

May 4, 2015   Session #1 10:00am  -  Noon      To the held in the  Convocation Room - Friend Center.

                       Session # 2    1 pm - 3 pm  

Spring IW Poster Session. Two Semester/Thesis students may attend, but are not expected to present.
