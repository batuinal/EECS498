UTA Program

Brown's Computer Science TA program is unlike any other. It employs almost 200 undergradutes each semester, and every one of these students is given the chance to effect change, working closely with the professor, other TAs, and other students. TAs and non-TAs alike can use this page to better understand the TA program, what it has to offer, and what a TA has the opportunity to do.

The Meta TAs for 2014-2015 are Aisha Ferrazares (administrative), Indy Prentice (technical), Emma Herold, and Eli Rosenthal.
