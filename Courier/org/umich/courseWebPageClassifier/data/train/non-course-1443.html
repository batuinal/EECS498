    CONNECTING WITH CAMPUS

    Outstanding students, world-class faculty and cutting-edge research are reasons why companies of all kinds forge relationships with U-M. If your company needs to hire one engineer or 1000, or if you need a new technical solution to enhance your competitiveness, our Corporate Relations staff can help you navigate our complex campus and pinpoint the resources that can be put to work for you.

    The Corporate Relations Office actively seeks—and welcomes—new connections to companies large and small. We work continuously to find fresh perspectives and solutions to address the challenges of our industry partners. Please contact us when you would like to discuss how Michigan Engineering can help your organization.
