Computer-Aided Design and VLSI

Research on computer-aided design (CAD) and very large-scale integrated (VLSI) circuits is conducted in the Advanced Computer Architecture Laboratory and the Solid-State Electronics Laboratory. EECS researchers have made seminal contributions in the areas of timing analysis and optimization, verification, reliable design, automatic test generation, and low-power design. Maintaining one of the largest CAD and VLSI research programs in the nation, EECS researchers are spearheading the exploration of novel design methodologies for trust-worthy and energy-efficient electronic systems in the nanometer regime.

Specialties

  • Design Verification and Validation
  • Logic Synthesis
  • Low-Power Circuits
  • Physical Design
  • Quantum Design Automation
  • Runtime Correctness
  • Test
