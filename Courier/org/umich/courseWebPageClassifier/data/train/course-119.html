The Atlantic Council Cyber 9/12 Student Challenge

The Atlantic Council runs a competition every year to provide students with "a better understanding of the policy challenges associated with cyber conflict."

Four Brown students who took this course in 2013 competed in the 2014 challenge made it to the second round and won the prize for Best Teamwork.

Four graduates of this course participated in the 2015 challenge and won first place!
