Dates & Deadlines - Single Semester IW

The following is a list of the important dates & deadlines for single semester IW students.

The Fall semester is over, click to jump to the spring semester ➔

Fall Semester (COS 397 / COS 497)

September 10 (✓ Completed)

Classes begin. Look here for project ideas.

September 11 (✓ Completed)

IW/thesis Signup Deadline.  Hard copies of your IW/thesis signup forms are due, signed by your faculty advisor, in the UGC'S office.

September 15, 4:30 pm, CS 105 (✓ Completed)

Welcome meeting. Pizza will be served. Attendance is MANDATORY.

September 22 - 26, CS 210 (Deadline is 4:30pm Sep 26) (✓ Completed)

Sign ups for IW/Thesis proposal presentations/progress reports will be done via WASS (Web Appointment Scheduling System). The UGC will provide the link to the calendars via email.

October 6 - 10, location TBD (✓ Completed)

Proposal presentations/progress reports given this week.

Please upload a .pdf of your presentation here:

https://dropbox.cs.princeton.edu/COSIW_F2014/Proposal_presentation

Upload a .pdf of your talk by 8am the morning of your talk. Please name your slide as follows:

DayOfPresentation_FirstInitial_Lastname.pdf

(EG for Maria Smith presenting on Wednesday it would be: Wednesday_M_Smith.pdf)

Students are expected to attend for an entire 1-hour block (starting and ending on the hour) and give feedback to other students in that time period.

Consult with your advisor in advance if they would like to attend.

→ November 7, 4:30 pm, CS 210   (✓ Completed)

Fall Semester Checkpoint Report Form, signed by your advisor due to the UGC - This can be sent via email along with your checkpoint report or you can deliver it to room 210.

E-mail a PDF copy of your checkpoint report to iwcoord (@lists.cs.princeton.edu) with a subject line of "FALL 2014 Checkpoint Report FirstName LastName" (with your name in place of FirstName LastName)

January 6, 4:30 pm - Final Paper Due       (✓ Completed)

Final electronic copy in PDF. Please do the following three things: (1) email a copy to ckenny@cs.princeton.edu (2) send a copy to your advisor and (3) upload a .pdf of your paper here:

https://dropbox.cs.princeton.edu/COSIW_F2014/Final_paper

Please name your paper as follows:

last name_first name.pdf

(✓ Completed)

January 12, 10:00am - 1:00pm, Poster Session , Carl Fields Center - Multipurpose Room  (

Fall IW Poster Session. (show up by 9:45am at the latest to set up your poster) IW students required to present. 

Please provide an electronic copy of your poster by 9:30am. Please upload a .pdf of your poster here:

https://dropbox.cs.princeton.edu/COSIW_F2014/Poster

Please name your pdf file as follows:

Firstname_Lastname.pdf

For consideration for the Poster Hall of Fame, please also upload a source file that allows us to print the poster at full-scale. This could be ppt or pptx in most cases.

Please name your additional file:

HallofFame_Firstname_Lastname.pptx

 

Spring Semester ( COS 398 / COS 498)

(✓ Completed)

January 9, 4:30pm, CS 210

IW Signup Deadline. Hard copies of your IW signup forms are due, signed by your faculty advisor, in the UGC's office. Feel free to email and meet with potential advisers to discuss project ideas. The earlier you find an adviser and get started on your research, the better.

Look here for project ideas.

February 2

Classes begin.

(✓ Completed)

February 6, 12:30pm - 1:30pm, CS 105

Welcome meeting. Pizza will be served. Attendance is MANDATORY. (slides)

(✓ Completed)

February 9 - 13, CS 210 (Deadline is 4:30pm Friday Feb 14)

Sign ups for IW proposal presentations/progress reports will be done via WASS (Web Appointment Scheduling System). The UGC will provide the link to the calendars via email.

February 23 - Feb 26, Prof. Narayana   /     March 2 - 4, Prof. Finkelstein

Proposal presentations/progress given this week.

Please upload a .pdf of your presentation here:

https://dropbox.cs.princeton.edu/COSIW_S2015/ProposalPresentation

Upload a .pdf of your talk by 8am the morning of your talk. Please name your slide as follows:

DayOfPresentation_FirstInitial_Lastname.pdf

(EG for Maria Smith presenting on Monday it would be: Monday_M_Smith.pdf)

Students are expected to attend for an entire 1-hour block (starting and ending on the hour) and give feedback to other students in that time period.

Consult with your advisor in advance if they would like to attend.

March 27, 4:30pm, CS 210

Spring Semester Checkpoint Report and Form, signed by your advisor due to the UGC

Also, do the following two things:

(1) E-mail a PDF copy of your checkpoint report to ckenny (@cs.princeton.edu) with a subject line of "SPRING 2015 Checkpoint-1 Report FirstName LastName" (with your name in place of FirstName LastName) (2) upload a .pdf of your paper here:

https://dropbox.cs.princeton.edu/COSIW_S2015/Checkpoint1

Please name your document as follows:

Firstname_Lastname.pdf

April 30, 4:30pm - Final Paper Due Date

Final electronic copy - mailed to your advisor and ckenny (@cs.princeton.edu) and uploaded at

https://dropbox.cs.princeton.edu/COSIW_S2015/FinalPaper

Monday, May 4, 2015 - Poster Session   Convocation Room - Friend Center

Session # 1  Time: 10:00am - Noon,   (Session # 1 show up by 9:45am at the latest to set up your poster) 

Session # 2   Time:  1 pm  -  3 pm       (Session # 2 show up by 12:45 pm at the latest to set up your poster)

Poster Session.

(Session #1 show up by 9:45am at the latest to set up your poster)     (Session #2 show up by 12:45 pm at the lastest to set up your poster)

Please provide an electronic copy of your poster by 9:30am. Please upload a .pdf of your poster here:

https://dropbox.cs.princeton.edu/COSIW_S2015/PosterPresentation

Please name your poster as follows:

Firstname_Lastname.pdf
