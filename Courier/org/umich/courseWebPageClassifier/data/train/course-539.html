WHAT IS CS 91SI: DIGITAL CANVAS?

Visual design is an essential skill for anyone who wants to make a product, especially a digital one. In this class, the spotlight's on you — and what you make. Through the quarter, you'll work on your very own web-focused visual design projects. The course will provide you with the framework you need to build something great — that means peer reviews, technical guidance, and an environment for you to focus on design. In addition, you'll have the opportunity to interact with professional designers, and through your project, build your professional portfolio.
