COURSE DESCRIPTION

Quantum graphs are idealized models of quantum systems on linear, network-shaped structures of vertices connected by edges.

The aim of the course is to obtain quantitative and qualitative information about the spectrum of Schroedinger operators on such structures, in particular, about the distribution of eigenvalues and resonances and about their continuous spectrum. We discuss the relevant background on one-dimensional Schroedinger operators in detail. Student presentations will form a part of the course.
