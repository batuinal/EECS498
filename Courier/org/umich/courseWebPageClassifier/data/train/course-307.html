COURSE DESCRIPTION

The third term of Ma 147 will be an introduction to holomorphic dynamics in one complex variable.

We will study basic facts concerning the dynamics of rational functions and Kleinian groups (and some other relevant dynamical systems).

This course will be essentially independent of Ma 147a and Ma 147b.

PREREQUISITES

basic complex analysis

SCHEDULE
