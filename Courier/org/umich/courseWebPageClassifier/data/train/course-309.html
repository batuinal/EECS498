POLICIES

Grading

There will be one take-home midterm and one take-home final, each counting 30% of the final grade. Homework will count 40% of the final grade. All homework and exams must be turned in by 2pm on the due date to the homework box of Ma 151c outside 253 Sloan. One free extension is acceptable. Collaboration is allowed for the homework but not allowed for the exams.
